About Scientific

HKU BrainSTEM Scientific is a brand-new, peer-reviewed, independent student research publication featuring the extraordinary scientific findings of HKU students. It is initiated by HKU Mainland STEM student associations (BrainSTEM) and sincerely welcomes any creative study or research outcomes around HKU. We highlight academic endeavors and self-exploration.
