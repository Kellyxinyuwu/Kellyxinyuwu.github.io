About BrainSTEM

Introduction of BrainSTEM

BrainSTEM is the abbreviated name for the HKU Mainland STEM student association, which was founded in 2019. Thanks to the tireless efforts of our first four executive teams, BrainSTEM has now grown to include over a thousand enthusiastic students who share a favor for Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics. Our mission is to provide support to all HKU STEM students in their academic pursuits, research endeavors, professional networking and career development.

Objectives

  1. Build an HKU STEM information database (courses, academic resources etc.)
  2. Enhance communication between students, professors, industry and the university
  3. Promote students’ academic, research and career development

Recent Activities

  1. Career talk (Oct, 2022):
    We were pleased to invite the CEO of the Friends of the Earth to conduct a career talk on green finance, sustainable technology and interdisciplinary career development in NGOs.

  2. Upper-level course information and experience sharing series (January, 2023):
    We compiled information on upper-level courses in MATH, STAT, CHEM, BIOL and COMP to help students select suitable courses from a wide range of options.

  3. Senior student research sharing session (February, 2023):
    We invited five senior undergraduates and three postgraduates (MPhil and PhD) to share their experiences in university-wise research program or postgraduate studies. This sharing session has received positive feedback from many fellow students who found the first-hand experiences and information across multiple disciplines to be highly useful. We also wrote a review post and a post on Summer Research Fellowship Program.

  4. Co-learning club (Phase I: March – April, 2023; Phase II: June – August, 2023):
    We established co-learning groups in several disciplines and gathered students with similar study interests. In the co-learning club, students met weekly to share self-explored knowledge beyond the classroom and discuss together. The first phase of co-learning club received high remark from participants and we decided to start a second phase in the summer vacation.

  5. AI study tool introduction series (May & June, 2023):
    We provided hands-on guidance on how to use ChatGPT, Image Generation, Canva AI and word as the study tools in the new information era.

  6. HKU BrainSTEM Scientific Student Journal (June – September, 2023) (Ongoing)

  7. Freshman Orientation Activities (August – September, 2023) (Upcoming)
