Submission Guidelines

Expectations：

  1. Subjects include science, technology, engineering, mathematics, and their related applications, such as business, medicine, healthcare, urban design, and sustainable development. 
  2. The primary aim is to spread knowledge, share insights and encourage communication across disciplines.
  3. The format is flexible, including but not limited to academic essays or reports, popular science articles, literature reviews, paper reading notes, reports of self-designed experiments, history of science development, case studies of applied technology, summary of interviews, and so forth.
  4. Articles that can be directly submitted to SCI journals are not recommended, such as those highly technical or theoretical or with a large amount of experiment results.

Requirements：

  1. Clear subjects, concentrated writing and concise language. 
  2. Organization can either be:
    a. Strictly follow the requirements of formal academic writing, including title, abstract, introduction, related literature, method, result, discussion, conclusion, etc.
    b. Choose an appropriate format based on the chosen topics, such as case studies, popular scientific articles, interview summaries, etc. However, please make sure the main idea and organization is clear.
  3. The expected Length is around 1200 to 2500 words. Being slightly longer or shorter is acceptable. Please use Times New Roman, size 12, 1.0 line space.
  4. In bilingual mode, we accept both Chinese and English initial submissions. The final publication on the website and summer issue will be in English, and the publicity on social media will be in Chinese.
  5. Both single author and cooperation are accepted.
  6. Please use proper citation and reference format. We recommend APA7 format and using [1][2] to indicate citation.

Method:

  1. We are now accepting submissions until September.
  2. In the following questionnaire, please fill in the author’s name, UID, contact email and submission title, and recommended review (optional).
  3. Please zip the manuscript, figures, tables, and appendices in one file and send it to stemhku@gmail.com. Please indicate the author’s name and submission titles in the email.
  4. Once the email and questionnaire are received, the editor team will reply to a successful submission notification and start processing the submission.

Writing Guidelines – How to get started:

  1. Find an area you are interested in, collect related information, and propose a question.
  2. Find ways to solve the problem by reading papers, studying online courses, designing algorithms or experiments, asking professors or senior students, etc. 
  3. Summarize findings in concise and scientific language.

Additional notes:

  1. Our journal is a non-profit student journal. It does not involve any procession fees or remuneration.
  2. Once accepted, the editor team has the right of distribution and reproduction of the electronic version of the manuscript.
  3. Articles should strictly follow the academic requirement. Citations and references should be written in a proper way. Plagiarism is forbidden.
